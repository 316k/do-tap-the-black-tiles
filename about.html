About [back]

Do tap the black tiles is a clone of Piano tiles : don't tap the white tiles designed for Firefox OS, but available on any device that can run a web browser.

It is developed by Nicolas Hurtubise (aka me) during his free time. The original game is not free (as in freedom) and comes with advertisement.

You should never accept to install such games on your phone or computer. It's simply insulting.

Some might say that "developers have to earn money to live even when they give away their games for free", but hey, look at me, I'm alive ! It's called having a dayjob instead of annoying people with brainwashing advertisement.

If you have ideas for new modes or for anything that could improve the game, let me know !

This game is distributed under the MIT license, and is therefore free software. You can contribute to it directly on Github.

If you've got time to waste, I made more games.
